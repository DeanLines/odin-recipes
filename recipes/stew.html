Butter Bean & Chorizo Stew

Description

A hearty stew to feed a family with just four ingredients.
Spicy chorizo and fresh pesto provide tons of flavour and it's on the table in just 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g cooking chorizo
  • 2 x 400g drained butter beans
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tub fresh pesto

Steps

  1. Slice the chorizo and tip into a large saucepan over a medium heat
  2. Fry gently for 5 mins or until starting to turn dark brown
  3. Add the tomatoes and butter beans, bring to the boil, then simmer for 10mins
  4. Swirl through the pesto, season lightly and ladle into four bowls
